◆ AdaptiveDeterminant()

float Engine.Physics.BEPUutilities.Matrix3x3.AdaptiveDeterminant ( out int  subMatrixCode)
package

Calculates the determinant of largest nonsingular submatrix, excluding 2x2's that involve M13 or M31, and excluding all 1x1's that involve nondiagonal elements.

Parameters
subMatrixCodeRepresents the submatrix that was used to compute the determinant. 0 is the full 3x3. 1 is the upper left 2x2. 2 is the lower right 2x2. 3 is the four corners. 4 is M11. 5 is M22. 6 is M33.
Returns
The matrix's determinant.
