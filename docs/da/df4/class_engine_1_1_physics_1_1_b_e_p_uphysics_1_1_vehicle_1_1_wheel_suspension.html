Detailed Description

Allows the connected wheel and vehicle to smoothly absorb bumps.

Constructs a new suspension for a wheel.

Parameters
stiffnessConstantStrength of the spring. Higher values resist compression more.
dampingConstantDamping constant of the spring. Higher values remove more momentum.
localDirectionDirection of the suspension in the vehicle's local space. For a normal, straight down suspension, this would be (0, -1, 0).
restLengthLength of the suspension when uncompressed.
localAttachmentPointPlace where the suspension hooks up to the body of the vehicle.
