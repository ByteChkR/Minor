Detailed Description

Prevents the connected entities from twisting relative to each other. Acts like the angular part of a universal joint.

Constructs a new constraint which prevents the connected entities from twisting relative to each other. To finish the initialization, specify the connections (ConnectionA and ConnectionB) as well as the WorldAxisA and WorldAxisB (or their entity-local versions).
