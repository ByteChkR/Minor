Handles any special logic when two bodies are touching and generate a contact point. Unlike ContactCreatedEventHandler, this will trigger at the time of contact generation instead of at the end of the space's update. This allows the contact's data to be adjusted prior to usage in the velocity solver, but other actions such as altering the owning space's pair or entry listings are unsafe.

otherOther entry within the pair opposing the monitored entry.

contactNewly generated contact point between the pair's two bodies. This reference cannot be safely kept outside of the scope of the handler; contacts can quickly return to the resource pool.
