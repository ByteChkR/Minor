enum Engine.Physics.BEPUphysics.Character.MovementMode
strong

Defines different movement states associated with the character.

Enumerator
Traction 

The character has controlled contact with a support object. The character can move normally.

Sliding 

The character has uncontrolled contact with a support object. The character should have limited control over its movement. Imagine sliding down a steep slope.

Floating 

The character has no support.

◆ Stance

enum Engine.Physics.BEPUphysics.Character.Stance
strong

Stance of a cylindrical character.

Enumerator
Standing 

Tallest stance.

Crouching 

Middle-height stance; must be taller than prone and shorter than standing.

Prone 

The shortest stance; the character is essentially on the ground. Note that the width and length of the character do not change while prone.
