◆ MinimumDownStepHeight

float Engine.Physics.BEPUphysics.Character.StepManager.MinimumDownStepHeight
getset

Gets or sets the minimum down step height. Down steps which are smaller than this are simply ignored by the step system; instead, the character falls. If the new step location has traction, the intermediate falling will not remove traction from the character. The only difference is that the character isn't teleported down when the step is too small.
