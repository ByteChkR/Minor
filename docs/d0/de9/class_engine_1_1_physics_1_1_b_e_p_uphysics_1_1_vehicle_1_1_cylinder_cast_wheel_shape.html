Parameters
radiusRadius of the wheel.
widthWidth of the wheel.
localWheelOrientationUnsteered orientation of the wheel in the vehicle's local space.
localGraphicTransformLocal graphic transform of the wheel shape. This transform is applied first when creating the shape's worldTransform.
includeSteeringTransformInCastWhether or not to include the steering transform in the wheel shape cast. If false, the casted wheel shape will always point straight forward. If true, it will rotate with steering. Sometimes, setting this to false is helpful when the cast shape would otherwise become exposed when steering.
