Detailed Description

Volume in which physically simulated objects have a buoyancy force applied to them based on their density and volume.

surfaceTrianglesList of triangles composing the surface of the fluid. Set up as a list of length 3 arrays of Vector3's.
depthDepth of the fluid back along the surface normal.
