Detailed Description

Constrains the relative orientation of two entities to within an ellipse.

◆ EllipseSwingLimit() [1/3]

Engine.Physics.BEPUphysics.Constraints.TwoEntity.JointLimits.EllipseSwingLimit.EllipseSwingLimit ( )

Constructs a new swing limit. To finish the initialization, specify the connections (ConnectionA and ConnectionB) as well as the TwistAxis (or its entity-local version), the MaximumAngleX and MaximumAngleY, and the Basis. This constructor sets the constraint's IsActive property to false by default.

Parameters
connectionAFirst entity connected by the constraint.
connectionBSecond entity connected by the constraint.
twistAxisAxis in world space to use as the initial unrestricted twist direction. This direction will be transformed to entity A's local space to form the basis's primary axis and to entity B's local space to form its twist axis. The basis's x and y axis are automatically created from the twist axis.
maximumAngleXMaximum angle of rotation around the basis X axis.
maximumAngleYMaximum angle of rotation around the basis Y axis.
